Day Three (Atlanta)

Posted on Sat 08 October 2016 in Eitan Posts

12 hours, and a lot of rain later...

Atlanta arrival

A video posted by Eitan Romanoff (@eitanromanoff) on Oct 7, 2016 at 9:11pm PDT

...We successfully arrived in Atlanta, dodging hurricane Matthew. The trip was mildly beautiful, when the skies weren't completely overcast. There were many religious billboards, more than I was expecting, which was a surprise. Food consisted of on-the-road offerings, including a stop at Chik-fil-A, since I've never been. It was pretty good, though the flavor lacked a notable taste of social equality. Overall, a lot of driving, and not a whole lot of other stuff. The price of a road trip that is "only" a month long.

I decided that since I'm actually getting over my newest flavor of contagion, I'd crash the daily maskhouse IT team scrum meeting. My manager affirmed that I am indeed a dedicated employee. I mean, we don't see Gary showing up to scrum meetings during his vacation, just saying.

At some point, Aron's favorite song came on. Not safe for work language, obviously.

New Orleans is one of my most anticipated stops!
